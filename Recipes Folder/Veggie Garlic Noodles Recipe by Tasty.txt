Veggie Garlic Noodles
This simple and satisfying recipe features tender noodles tossed in garlic, soy sauce, and sesame oil,
then topped with vegetables for a flavorful vegetarian meal.

Tasty Team

Ingredients
for 4 servings
2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 cup snap peas

5 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons brown sugar

4 green onions, sliced, divided

3 tablespoons soy sauce

2 carrots, cut into matchsticks

9 oz dried rice noodles, cooked
