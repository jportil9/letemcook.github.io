Easy Butter Chicken
This simplified version of the Indian classic combines chicken, tomato sauce, and a slew of aromatic
spices all in one pot to make a flavorful dinner that’s just as good as the version you’ll get at restaurants
— only way easier to make. Serve it over rice with a bit of cilantro to balance the heat and dinner is
done.

Preparation
1

In a large bowl, season the chicken with salt, pepper, 1 teaspoon of chili powder, and the
turmeric. Let sit for 15 minutes to marinate.

2

Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a large pot over medium heat. Brown the chicken, then
remove from the pot.

3

Melt another 2 tablespoons of butter in the pot, then add the onion, garam masala,
remaining teaspoon of chili powder, the cumin, ginger, garlic, cayenne, cinnamon, salt, and
pepper. Cook until fragrant.

4

Add the tomato sauce and bring to a simmer.

5

Add the water and cream and return to a simmer.

6

Return the chicken to the pot, cover, and simmer for 10-15 minutes.

7

Stir in the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and season with more salt and pepper to taste

8

Serve the chicken over rice and garnish with cilantro.

9

Enjoy!
