Bacon And Egg Ramen Recipe by Tasty

This ramen is an egg-cellent twist on a classic dish, with savory bacon and perfectly cooked eggs. The broth is a flavor bomb, making every slurp more delicious than the last. It's a quick and easy recipe that will have you coming back for more!
Matthew Johnson
Tasty Team

Updated on August 14, 2023
Under 30 minutes

Ingredients for 1 serving
1 package instant ramen
1 tablespoon thinly sliced scallions
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
kosher salt, to taste
1 fried egg
black pepper, to taste
3 slices bacon, cooked and chopped

Preparation

1. In a small pot, bring 2 cups (480 ml) of water to a boil. Add the ramen seasoning packet an
a tablespoon of butter. Stir until the butter melts and then add the ramen noodles and cook
until tender, 2 minutes. Pour the ramen into a bowl.
2. Top the ramen with the fried egg, bacon, and scallions and season with salt and pepper.
3. Enjoy!
